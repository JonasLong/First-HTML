If you've heard about the new Iphone 7, you're probably wondering what new features it has. In addition to added security features, the Iphone 7 will make use of the entire front of the phone, with no margins around the screen. The phone also features a new adapter and charging cord featuring inductive charging. The Iphone 7 additionally showcases wireless earbuds for a tangle free listening experience. According to Apple, the phones "should not explode" like the Samsung phones that came out recently. Apple is ramping up its order from factories to take advantgage of the fact that many people seem to be unhappy about the tendency of the samsung phones to explode. However, this could end with Apple loosing money if enough people want to keep their galaxy phones despite the recall. If this happens, Apple might loose the money they invested on the extra phones.
